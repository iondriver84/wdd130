Overview

Purpose

My purpose is to provide Magic: The Gathering Commander players the ability to create custom labels using templates for their Magic: The Gathering art cards, in order for them to be able to use their art cards in their games.

Audience

My audience is primarily Magic: The Gathering players, and more specifically Commander players (Commander is the most popular format within the game).
