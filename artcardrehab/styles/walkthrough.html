Walkthrough

Instructions on how to turn art cards into playables!

Order Now

Transforming your art cards into playables is a breeze with these simple steps:

Carefully cut out your sticker from the sticker sheet with a hobby knife

peel the label and apply to the card carefully - take your time!

Gently smooth out any air bubbles

Enjoy your finished cards in your commander decks!
